Frequently Asked Questions

What is ISSN of ISJCRESM?

  • ISSN: 2456-1134

How do I submit my paper to ISJCRESM?

  • Please submit your paper to chiefeditor.isjcresm@gmail.com.

What is the Impact Factor of ISJCRESM?

  • Impact Factor of ISJCRESM by Scientific Journal Impact Factor (SJIF) is 0.9.

What is the Thomson Reuters Researcher ID of ISJCRESM?

  • Refer Indexing page

When will I get acknowledgement of Paper Submission?

  • Immediately, you will get the mail with paper id.

When can I expect the paper acceptance mail?

  • You will get the Notification of acceptance within 48 hours of paper submission.

What should I do in the case of delay?

  • We will try to resolve your issue as per promised time. You can send SMS or Whatsapp on +91-96863-08745 in the case of any issue.

How much time is required for Publication of Paper after paying the publication of fee?

  • We will publish your paper within 48 hours after receiving the Copyright form and Publication Fee.

How can I check the status of my Paper?

  • You can check status of your paper on our website with paper id.

How will I came to know about the Publication of my paper?

  • You will get a mail from ISJCRESM Team.

How Can I download my certificates and paper?

  • You can download your paper and certificates from past issues or you can download the same with paper id from the link

What is the Publication Fee?

  • Publication fee for Indian Authors is ₹ 2000. Fee for the International Authors is $ 100 USD.

Will you provide Hard Copies to all Authors?

  • Yes, we do provide the Hard Copies to all authors on their postal address.

Is there any extra charges for Soft Copy of Certificates, Hard Copy of Certificates and Courier?

  • No, there is no extra hidden charges. Everything is Included in the Publication Fee.

Do you provides the Crossref DOI?

  • Yes, we are providing DOI to all articles.

How to pay publication fee?

  • You can pay publication fee online through our website or offline.

How many Authors are allowed in a Single Paper?

  • You can publish the paper with any number of the authors.

Can I call on ISJCRESM helpline number?

  • Yes, you can call us but only for the Emergency Issues. You are advised to read FAQ's or you can send us the SMS/Whatsapp on our Mobile number +91-96863-08745. Our team will try to resolve your query asap.

Is there any particular format of ISJCRESM?

  • Yes, We do have the paper format for ISJCRESM You can download it from our website.

Can I know about the ISJCRESM Indexing?

  • Yes, you can visit our website for more details about the Indexing.
