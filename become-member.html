Become a Member

Apply For Reviewer/ Editorial/ Advisory Board

Our journal ISJCRESM which is an open access and peer-review are looking for Reviewers to review the paper. Only the Reviewers and editors determine which works are of quality and significance.

Due to our widespread readership, the research and scholarship selected by our reviewers will eventually have an impact on literacy in national and international classrooms.

Reviewer's names will be listed in the printed journal and on the journal's webpage.

Requirements

Reviewers should possess a doctoral degree in a discipline related closely to the journal

Must hold a teaching or research position at a university or academic institution

Be fluent in academic and professional English

Have a strong interest in the scholarly journal

Work quickly and accurately under tight deadlines

How to apply

High profiled experts in their respective field of research are invited for advisory editorial members/referees/reviewers. They can send their biodata with their field of interest to the following email chiefeditor.isjcresm@gmail.com by filling form Join Editorial Board Form The Advisory editorial members/referees/reviewers are honorary position where their contributions to the journal would be acknowledged in each issue.
